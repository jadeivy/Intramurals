NOVEMBER 20-22 | 2018 INTRAMURALS
ZUMBA DAY

Before the start of Intramurals 2018 in Christ the King College, zumba to all the students and even the teachers is done.

DAY 1

First day of the Intramurals was intense. The first thing done was a race of raising banners of each team. Then there was an activity where participants are called to represent and yell out their team's goal. In the afternoon, dancers from every grade level showed their talents in performing the variety show.

DAY 2

A sporty day. The school held sports activity especially ball games like basketball and volleyball, badminton, and obstacle race for the students and teachers. There were also board games like scrabble and chess.

DAY 3

Even the last day of Intramurals 2018 was still hot and exciting. This day, the representatives from each team will make a move to win the title of Ambassador and Ambassadress of Health and Wellness. Not just to the students from every team but also the stunning and awesome teachers who represented their grade levels.
